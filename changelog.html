Changelog

This changelog only tracks substantive rules changes (those sufficient enough that I think they might result in the change of a build). For changes to code, or small phrasings, see my github.

November 8, 2018
  • Added Summer Elf species.
November 7, 2018
  • Updated requirements of Talents Grit and Toughened.
November 3, 2018
  • Changed Career Skill for Scholar: Cool became Mechanics.
  • Changed Career Skill for Scoundrel: Charm became a choice between Charm and Computers.
  • Removed Socialite Career until I can figure out how to differentiate the role from Envoy.
November 2, 2018
  • Added Talents Identify Weakness (Tier 2), and Exploit Weakness (Tier 1).
  • Reworked Hacker Career Skills. Their Career Skills are now: Computers, Mechanics, Perception, Piloting, Streetwise, Vigilance, (Skulduggery or Stealth), (Any Knowledge).
  • Changed Red Magic's Strength Skill from Science to Discipline.
November 1, 2018
  • Removed Tier 3 Talent Overwhelm Defenses, as it didn't make sense with the Genesys vehcle rules changes.
  • Changed Explorer Career's combat skill choice to 'Any Combat'.
  • Changed Wizard Career's Coercion into a choice, along with Deception.
October 30, 2018
  • Added Utility magic spell to Magic.
  • Added the Tier 2 Talent Freerunning.
October 29, 2018
  • Added Unto the Breach and Adaptable to Talents.
October 28, 2018
  • Added Fatale and Sense Emotions to Talents.

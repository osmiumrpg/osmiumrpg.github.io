Manufacturers

A listing and description of who makes stuff, because nothing makes a sci-fi game really sing like branding.

Dark Ephemeris Smithwerks

A Dwarven and Gnomish weapons maker in the Hegemony known for their exacting craftsmanship. Their weapons are designed with a very strong Dwarven standpoint. The tactical assumptions they make are informed by the battles they have fought. Often black and either bright yellow or gold in color.

Osmium

Galaxies of Fantasy Adventure

The Osmium Universe is one of infinite possibilities, a kitchen-sink where awesome is dialed to 11. The GM should be a fan of their players, and the players should be excited to play in the universe, so the universe has to be epic enough for the players to feel truly grandiose against the backdrop.
